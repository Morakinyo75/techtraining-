The Internet is a global network of interconnected computers and devices that communicate using standardized protocols. It enables the exchange of information, data, and services across the world. 
The Internet has become an integral part of modern life, serving as a platform for innovation, connectivity, and the exchange of ideas on a global scale.

Information on the Internet moves from one computer to another in the form of bits over various mediums, including Ethernet cables, fiber optic cables, and wireless signals (i.e., radio waves).
The information that is being transfered now communicates with other computers through a protocol using the IP Address of each computer. 

Taking about packets, routing and reliability, the information transfer is done in the form of packets and these packets may follow different routes depending on certain factors.

HTTP is the standard protocol by which webpages are transferred over the Internet.

When we talk about Encryption and Public Keys, this is how informations are being kept safe while being transmitted

Cybersecurity refers to the protective measures against criminal activity accomplished through using a network, technological devices, and the internet.
